About Japan

Japan (Japanese: 日本, Nippon or Nihon, and formally 日本国, Nippon-koku or Nihon-koku) is an island country in East Asia. Japan is a part of the Ring of Fire, and spans an archipelago of 14,125 islands, with the five main islands being Hokkaido, Honshu (the "mainland"), Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa. Tokyo is the country's capital and largest city, followed by Yokohama, Osaka, Nagoya, Sapporo, Fukuoka, Kobe, and Kyoto.
