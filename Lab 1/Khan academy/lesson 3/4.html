Spaghetti Bolognese
  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 2

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

Spaghetti 250g
Ground beef 300g
Tomato sauce 1 cup

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Brown ground beef in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the beef and simmer.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

Fettuccine pasta 250g
Chicken breast 300g
Alfredo sauce 1 cup

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Cook chicken breast and shred it.
  3. Mix shredded chicken with Alfredo sauce and toss with cooked pasta.
